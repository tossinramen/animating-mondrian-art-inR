High level goal: Our main goal with this project is to explore how Mondrian’s art can be illustrated and transformed with ggplot2.

A one to two paragraph description of your goals, including your motivation.

We hope to explore the relationships between Mondrian’s works by transforming each piece into the next. We’ll do this by animating the process of expanding boxes, changing positions and colors. Our motivation for this project stems from our fascination with Mondrian’s ability to convey aesthic meanings through minimalistic compositions, along with our desire to learn how to animate with ggplot2, something none of us have yet explored.

One of the major goals we have while working on this project is to offer a new perspective on interpreting art with data visualization using ggplot2. By working on animating the transormation of one piece into a another we hope to drastically increase our skills in the visualization field of R while also visualizing Mondrian’s artistic journey and expertise through animating expanding boxes and changing positions. We hope that this process will help us uncover any patterns in Mondrian’s work and help us better understand the thought process Mondrian had while creating his work.
